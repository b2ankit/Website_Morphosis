About Morphosis

Technical Fest plays an important role in the college curriculum. It is one such event where young brains get to showcase their skills and compete with others to find the best. It is not just about the competitions but also an event where a lot of those new ideas are seen live and learn more and be inspired. Such inspiring events that happen every year in various colleges will guide engineers to dream bigger and make those dreams come true. Technical Fest these ideas to bring the bright minds in INDIA together in a pool so that their ideas can be mixed together to provide a solution for an existing substantial problem in the nation or world.
